Trent Teinert is a guy currently residing in the Lower East Side of Manhattan. His creative practice explores the distant connection to his sociological surroundings. As a wanderer in the streets of New York City, he observes people, objects, and how they navigate and interact with the world around them. He relies on people to draw him in by their movements and daily routines Finding a story to pull from his images, relating to codependency and the flux of human emotion…

Born 1998 / Harlingen, Tx.
