Is a photographer currently residing in the Lower East Side of Manhattan. His current work falls in between portraiture and landscape, working as environmental portraits on the street of NY. relying on happenstance and will to find ways of connecting with his surroundings. Relying on the people to draw him in by their movement and routine. Distracting the mind and falling into sync with the environment.
